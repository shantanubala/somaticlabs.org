The notifications you need,

The Somatic Pulse delivers updates from your calendar, inbox, and GPS as a tactile sensation you feel in real-time.

Better interfaces through haptics

Instead of distracting your visual focus and attention, the Somatic Pulse uses tactile feedback directly on your skin to provide notifications and updates from your cell phone. Using a low-energy Bluetooth connection, it takes updates from your favorite apps and assigns them a unique sensation on your skin. Know who is calling you, how much time you have until your next meeting, and when to take a left turn - all without ever stopping to look at your phone.
